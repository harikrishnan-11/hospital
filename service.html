How It Works

From Booking to Recovery

A consistent four-step path, whether you're here for a routine consult or a scheduled procedure.

01

Book a Slot

Choose a department online, by app, or by calling our helpline — confirmed within 24 hours.

02

Consult & Diagnose

Meet your specialist, run any required diagnostics on-site, and get a clear treatment plan.

03

Treat

Proceed with treatment or surgery, with insurance pre-authorisation handled by our desk.

04

Recover & Follow Up

Track recovery and lab results through the Stackly Patient App, with scheduled follow-ups.
