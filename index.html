FAQ

Questions We
Hear Most Often

Can't find the answer here? Call our helpline at 1800-STACKLY or send us a message.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?
For most of our specialties — Cardiology, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, Dermatology, and more — you can book directly without a GP referral. A few specialist departments like Neurosurgery and Transplant Medicine prefer a referral. Our helpdesk can confirm this for your specific case when you call to book.
Which insurance providers do you accept?
We have cashless tie-ups with all major insurers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, Medi Assist, Apollo Munich, and New India Assurance. Our insurance desk verifies your policy and pre-authorises eligible treatments before your visit.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
Most appointment requests are confirmed within 24 hours. Urgent cases are prioritised and can often be seen the same day. Emergency cases are seen immediately, 24/7, with no appointment required.
Do you offer telemedicine consultations?
Yes, video consultations are available across most departments through the Stackly Patient App. Follow-ups, prescription renewals, and second opinions can all be handled remotely; in-person visits are recommended for new diagnoses requiring examination.
What support is available for international patients?
Our International Patient Desk handles visa invitation letters, airport pickup, accommodation referrals, and interpreter services in over a dozen languages. Pre-approved treatment packages with transparent pricing are available on request.
Can I access my reports and records online?
Yes, the Stackly Patient App gives you secure access to lab results, scan reports, prescriptions, and your full visit history. Reports are typically uploaded within 2 hours of critical tests and 24 hours for routine panels.
