FAQ

Package Questions

Can I split package tests across two visits?
Yes. Within the 12-month validity, you can complete the fasting blood tests on one visit and imaging-based tests on another if scheduling is easier for you.
Is fasting required before the tests?
Most blood panels require 10–12 hours of fasting. Our wellness desk will confirm exact fasting instructions when you schedule your appointment slot.
Can I upgrade to a higher package later?
Yes, you can upgrade within 30 days of your original booking by paying the price difference, provided testing hasn't already started.
Are these packages covered by insurance?
Preventive packages are typically not covered by insurance, but many corporate wellness plans and HSA-style benefits do reimburse them — check with your provider.
