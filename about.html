What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Three commitments that shape every decision we make, from the operating theatre to the front desk.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, evidence-based healthcare that treats every patient with the dignity, clarity, and urgency their condition deserves — regardless of background or means.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted destination for complex care, where clinical research and bedside compassion advance together, not as competing priorities.

Our Values

Integrity in diagnosis, transparency in cost, respect for every patient's time, and a standing commitment to keep learning as medicine evolves.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

From a 40-bed clinic to a 600-bed research hospital — the milestones that shaped Stackly.

1994

Stackly Clinic Opens

Founded in Teynampet, Chennai as a 40-bed general clinic serving the local community.

2002

Cardiac & Trauma Wing

Expanded to 150 beds with the region's first dedicated 24/7 cardiac catheterisation lab.

2010

NABH Accreditation

Became one of the first hospitals in Tamil Nadu to receive NABH Gold-level certification.

2016

Research Wing Launched

Opened a 40-lab research centre, partnering with ICMR on clinical trials and oncology studies.

2021

JCI Accreditation & 600 Beds

Achieved Joint Commission International accreditation alongside a major campus expansion.

2026

50,000+ Patients Treated

Crossed a major patient-care milestone while launching the Stackly Patient App for remote care.
